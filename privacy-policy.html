Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 15, 2026

Headlinica respects reader privacy. This policy explains how we handle information submitted through forms, newsletter signups, and cookie preferences.

Information We Collect

We may collect your name, email address, subject, message, interest selection, and consent confirmation when you submit a form. We also store your cookie choice in localStorage on your device.

How We Use Information

We use submitted information to respond to inquiries, manage newsletter interest, improve editorial services, and maintain site security. We do not sell personal information.

Cookies

The site includes a GDPR-style cookie banner with Accept, Reject, and Preferences choices. Your selection is stored locally so the banner does not reappear unnecessarily.

Your Choices

You may contact us to request correction or deletion of information you submitted. Browser settings can clear localStorage and reset cookie choices. To exercise rights related to your personal data (access, correction, deletion, portability), contact us at the address below and include sufficient details to process your request.

Analytics and Third-Party Services

We use third-party analytics and content-delivery services to understand site usage and improve performance. These services may collect usage data (IP address, pages visited, device and browser information) under their own privacy policies. Where possible we configure analytics to minimize collection of personal data.

Data Retention

We retain form submissions and newsletter signups for as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes. If you request deletion, we will remove personal identifiers from our active systems, subject to legal or operational constraints.

Security

We maintain administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access. No internet transmission is completely secure; if you have concerns about sending sensitive information, contact us by phone.

International Transfers

Headlinica may operate services and store data in multiple jurisdictions. If your data is transferred internationally we apply safeguards consistent with applicable law.

Children

Our site is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children; if you believe we have such data, contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be posted with an updated effective date. Continued use of the site after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
